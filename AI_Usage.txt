Throughout the development of this project, I used several AI models (like ChatGPT, gemeni, and claude)
All design decisions, implementation, testing, and final code integration were completed by me, 
while AI was used to assist in the following areas:

- Assisted with database design and schema planning.
- Helped construct and optimize complex SQL queries.
- Help apply consistent styling throughout the project.
- Assisted in making the interface responsive across different screen sizes and devices.
- Helped identify and debug difficult or unexpected issues.
- Reviewed Flask routes to identify potential bugs, edge cases,
  and security vulnerabilities, and suggested ways to mitigate them.
- Reviewed code and provided suggestions for readability, maintainability, and best practices.
